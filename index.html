About Us

Kerio Valley Conservancy (KVC) is a community-based organization addressing poverty, climate vulnerability, and environmental degradation in the Kerio Valley.

What We Do

We promote agroforestry, climate-smart agriculture, environmental conservation, and sustainable livelihoods.

Our Impact

Improved food security, restored landscapes, empowered communities, and increased household incomes.
